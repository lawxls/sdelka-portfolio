description: Interview the user relentlessly about a plan or design until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get interviewed on their design, or mentions "interview me".
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

- Explore the codebase before beginning the interview process.
- Ask the questions one at a time, waiting for feedback on each question before continuing.
- Use AskUserQuestion tool.
- Don't ask questions which can be answered by exploring the codebase.
